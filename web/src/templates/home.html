Key Value Proposition

Key Value Proposition

Our plant monitoring system, PlantPal, helps amateur gardeners who wish to learn proper plant maintenance for healthier plants by displaying soil moisture, sunlight, and temperature history on our app and sending reminders and tips for plant maintenance. Unlike passive maintenance systems such as automated sprinklers, our product actively reads plant data to provide more optimate caretaking. Additionally, our product acts as a learning tool for amateur hobbyists who want to learn more about gardening

Product Page

Product

PlantPal is a network of devices to monitor various factors such as soil moisture, sunlight levels, and temperature to provide optimal instructions for plant maintenance. PlantPal works by taking readings from capacitive PCB, a photoresistor, and a thermistor. The capacitive PCB will stick into the soil of a plant that needs monitoring and will read voltage outputs from a pulse. Depending on the moisture content of the soil, the capacitance in the circuit changes which will distort the pulse. From these distortions, we can extrapolate the moisture content of the soil. Similarly, we will use readings from a photoresistor and a thermistor to read sunlight levels and temperature. Our product will record the history of these variables and present them graphically on our UI. Based on these readings, our product will provide instructions via reminders through our app for plant maintenance such as watering, draining, or moving locations to optimize healthy plant growth.

Customer

This product is designed for amateur hobbyists who want to get into gardening. Nowadays plants are imported from all parts of the world. Each plant thrives in different conditions and as a novice gardener, it is difficult to determine the optimate conditions for each plant to prevent them from dying. Our product acts as a trainer by providing reminders, tips, and instructions for plant maintenance for beautiful, healthy plants.

Key Benefits

A key benefit of our product is active monitoring. Because our product is constantly reading plant growth variables we're able to provide optimal instruction for plant maintenance resulting in a healthier plant.

The devices are all connected to a centralized database which is viewable via our phone app. The product is designed to help individuals learn or improve their plant caretaking skills.

Week 3

Bluetooth

This week were were able to successfully connect the ESP32 board to bluetooth and communicate with our phones. We created a simple test to turn on an LED. We successfully paired the board to the phone's bluetooth and were able to toggle the LED light remotely.

Plans

Our plan for the next week is to work on the PCB for our product. The goal is to build a capacitive circuit that will stick into the soil and read voltage values. Depending on the soil's moisture content the capacitance of the circuit will change which should affect the voltage readings. Based on this well will determine the soil's moisture content.
