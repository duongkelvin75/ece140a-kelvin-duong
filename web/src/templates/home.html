Project Updates

Week 2

Product Proposal

We would like to introduct our product PlantPal. PlantPal is a set of devices read moisture, sunlight, and temperature data to monitor the health of your plants. The devices are all connected to a centralized database which is viewable via our phone app. The product is designed to help individuals learn or improve their plant caretaking skills.

Early Market Research

We presented our initial proposal to friends and family who engjoy gardening and asked wether or not they would be interested in buying a finished product. From our poll 11/12 would be interested in our product showing that it potential for our customer base. We also asked wether they saw the product as a vitamin or painkiller and the customers were fairly split with 58.3% claiming the product was vitamin while the other 41.7 claiming the product to be a pain killer.
